Description

Indulge in the ultimate comfort food with our classic mac and cheese. Made with perfectly cooked pasta and a rich, velvety cheese sauce, this dish delivers a warm, satisfying bite every time. Whether you're looking for a quick meal or a hearty side, our mac and cheese is a timeless favorite that's ready to please.

Making Mac and cheese has never been this simple, and this side dish pairs good with just about everythig. like some good southern fried chicken

Ingredients

  • 16 oz macaroni noodles
  • 1 cup ,ild cheddar cheese,shredded
  • 1/4 cup sharp cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1/4 cup Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
  • 1/4 cup Colby cheese, shredded
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 stick butter
  • salt, pepper, and peprika

Steps

  1. Wash hands with soap and water.

  2. Preheat oven to 350°F.

  3. Fill a large pot with 6 cups of hot water, a tablespoon of salt and bring to a boil.

  4. Place macaroni into boiling water and cook for 12 minutes, remove and strain in a colander. Set aside. Return the empty pot to the stove.

  5. Crack two eggs in a small bowl and mix. Add eggs, butter, ½ cup mild cheddar cheese, ¼ cups of sharp cheddar cheese, Monterey Jack and Colby cheeses into the empty pasta pot and mix over medium heat until slightly melted.

  6. Return the macaroni noodles to the same pot and mix well until cheese is fully melted.

  7. Pour the macaroni and cheese into a greased baking dish.

  8. Bake until the internal temperature reaches 165°F on a food thermometer. Then top with remaining shredded cheese and bake 5 minutes until the cheese is melted.

  9. Serve immediately
